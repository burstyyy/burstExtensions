burstHAR Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 May 2026

Overview

burstHAR is a Chrome extension for capturing, replaying, modifying, and exporting network activity as HAR files.

Data Collection

burstHAR may capture network request and response data from websites you choose to monitor. This can include URLs, request headers, response headers, request bodies, response bodies, status codes, and timing information.

How Data Is Used

Captured data is used only to provide the extension’s core functionality, including inspecting, replaying, modifying, saving, and exporting HAR traffic.

Data Storage

Captured HAR data is stored locally in your browser unless you choose to export or otherwise share it. burstHAR does not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

burstHAR may use third-party services for account management, payments, or licensing. These services are used only to provide extension functionality and subscription management.

User Control

You can clear captured HAR data from within the extension at any time.
